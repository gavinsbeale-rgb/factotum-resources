Weekly 121
Meeting Framework

A shared guide for HODs — what to prepare, what we cover, and what we take away from every weekly check-in.

Before
Prepare by end of Friday
  • Update your section of the spreadsheet with all live leads and their current status Wed 1 July for first report
  • Note all meetings held that week — client or company name, what they do, services discussed (in person and virtual)
  • Identify two potential cross-sell ideas from your week — doesn't need to be fully formed, just worth raising
  • Flag anything urgent via the HOD WhatsApp group ahead of the meeting
During
What we cover in the meeting
  • Pipeline review — walk through live leads; what's moving, what's blocked, and where the best immediate opportunities sit
  • Blockage and support — where can I help? Blue-sky thinking, resource pooling from other divisions, introductions
  • Meetings log — quick scan of the week's activity; flag anything with cross-sell potential for other HODs to be aware of
  • Cross-sell ideas — two ideas from you; I'll note which other divisions should be looped in
  • BD highlights — any tangible progress or wins on the back of actions from the previous meeting
After
What comes out of each round of 121s
  • Leads that can clearly move forwards — with named next actions and owners
  • Cross-sell opportunities flagged to the relevant HODs
  • A top-line BD highlight of tangible activity on the back of prior meeting actions
  • A brief all-HOD follow-up email pulling all of the above together
What this is for

The 121s are a space to review what's live, spot where things are getting stuck, and share opportunities across the group. The goal is simple: more consistent revenue, fewer hidden opportunities slipping through, and a stronger foundation for growth. The spreadsheet is a starting point — a refined version, a proper sales process, and a central CRM are all in the roadmap.
